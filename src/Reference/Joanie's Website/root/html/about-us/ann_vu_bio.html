Ann Yu, MD

Dr. Vu has been in practice in Newport Beach and South Orange County since the completion of her dermatology residency in 2000. She immigrated to the United States from Vietnam in 1975. Her family then moved to Nebraska for five years. In 1980, her family settled in Fresno where she completed high school. She was awarded a National Merit Scholarship as well as a Regent’s scholarship for her four years of undergraduate work at UC Davis. She graduated with honors with a Bachelor’s of Science in Physiology. Then it was off to the east coast where she received her M.D. at New York University. She completed her internship year as well as her dermatology residency at Yale University.

Dr. Vu has always wanted to come back to California so she jumped at the opportunity to settle in Orange County. Dr. Vu enjoys all aspects of dermatology and specializes in general medical dermatology, lasers, as well as cosmetic dermatology. She sees patients of all ages and loves the opportunity to be able to treat many generations within one family. She lives in Laguna Niguel with her husband who is a general surgeon and has four young children, two boys and two girls.

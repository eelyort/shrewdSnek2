Joannie Sun, MD

Dr. Sun grew up in Bozeman, MT, enjoying skiing, fishing and biking in her youth. She attended Duke University and graduated with a B.S. in Biology. She went on to obtain her medical degree from the University of Washington in 1996, and completed a full residency there in internal medicine as well. After completing her internal medicine residency, she decided to change specialties and completed a dermatology residency at UC-Irvine. She has been in private practice in dermatology since she completed her training.

Dr. Sun focuses primarily on medical dermatology, treating skin cancers, including performing Mohs microsurgery, as well as other general dermatology. She is interested in lasers for skin rejuvenation and other areas of cosmetic dermatology, such as Botox and fillers. She sees patients of all ages (including children) and enjoys the relationships that arise from seeing her patients.

She is married and has two young children, one boy and one girl. She enjoys cooking, piano, jogging and travel in her spare time.
